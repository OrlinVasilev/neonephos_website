# NeoNephos Meeting Series

The next IPCEI-CIS Workstream 2 Multi Cluster Kubernetes (MCK) meeting session will be held on the **17th of February from 10:00-11:30 am CET**. 

Agenda:
 
- 40 min: **IronCore Networking behind the scenes**: Discover the architecture of IronCore IaaS Networking, a high-performance Software Defined Networking solution designed to deliver robust virtual networking for modern data centers. This presentation takes you behind the scenes to reveal how the system leverages advanced technologies like DPDK and SmartNICs to ensure exceptional speed and scalability for both bare metal and virtual workloads. We will explain how the architecture achieves dynamic multi-tenancy and seamless Kubernetes integration, removing the need for complex physical switch reconfigurations.
- 40 min: **Katalis - Federation: Architecture and practical Demonstration**: In this session, we will present the federation initiative and the activities carried out together with other partners within the IPCEI. We will explain what the federation is, the context in which it was created, and how it operates from both an architectural and operational perspective. We will also describe the main benefits it enables for participating partners in terms of collaboration, resource and services sharing.
